All about the city

The city of Hyderabad is easily one of the most sought-after destinations to visit. The land of the Nizams is known for its palaces, places of historical significance or shopping arenas and obviously the food! There are quite a lot of places to visit in Hyderabad. You can check out the best places to visit in the city by visiting our places to visit site. Hyderabad is a modern city with some ancient tombs and sculptures. It is the best city for exploration. Be aware of these tips while visiting Hyderabad. You will have a safe visit if you follow these tips. Visit Hyderabad, have an amazing time!


  1. Best time to visit the city

    2. Usually, Hyderabad’s weather is normal, but when you are visiting there, make sure that you are carrying loose and cotton clothes because Hyderabad is hot during the summer. Monsoon here leads to huge rainfall and humidity. The best time to visit Hyderabad is in the winter because the weather is pleasant and best for exploration that is the months between october to march.

  2. Place to stay

    3. Due to covid, do prebooking of your hotels and plan your travel beforehand. It would be better to stay a place which is near to your travel destininations. Not to forget, try to cover through areas the if you are planning to visit the whole city. It would be better if you would hire your very own cab for the day and travel in that.

  3. Mode of travel

    4. The transportation in Hyderabad is really good. Buses are the best choice for the longer destination and autos for the shorter destination. Be aware of auto drivers; they sometimes charge you extra fees. This metro city has a lot to see. You will have a unique experience in buses and autos.

  4. Food Culture

    5. Hyderabad is a food paradise for South Indian Food lovers. It is so popular for its non-vegetarian dishes like Biryani and all. Spicy Venue, The best Biryani, Jonathan’s Kitchen, and Aish-The Park are the best food places. So don’t miss visiting there.Also,The local food in Hyderabad is so spicy. So ask them to make food a little less spicy than they cook, if spicy food is not your choice. On the other hand, if you are good with spicy food, enjoy the tastiest food ever!

  5. Language

    6. In Hyderabad, you might face a language problem. Here the people usually speak Telugu (Hyderabad’s local language). Only some people speak English and Hindi. All the signboards are written in Telugu. So it is better to get a guide in Hyderabad who knows Telugu, Hindi and English language very well. It will be the best choice for the exploration in the unknown city.
